Getting Dressed!

字母 E · 穿衣服
🕐 约12分钟 📖 适合1.5–2岁 🎯 5个核心词
001·A 002·B 003·C 004·D 005·E 006·F 007·G

👕 今天学习穿衣服的英语！每天早上穿衣服时就是最好的英语时间，让宝宝自己选衣服、自己穿，在生活中自然习得！

💡 本期目标：认识 5 个服饰词汇，学唱 1 首身体儿歌，掌握早晨穿衣场景

📚 模块一 · 今日核心词汇
Shirt /ʃɜːrt/
上衣/衬衫
💡 拿衣服时说 "Shirt! Put on your shirt!"
"Which shirt do you want? The red one or blue one?"
Pants /pænts/
裤子
💡 拿裤子时说 "Pants! Step into your pants!"
"These are your pants! Nice and comfy!"
Socks /sɒks/
袜子
💡 拿袜子时说 "Socks! One for this foot, one for that foot!"
"Put on your socks! This way!"
Shoes /ʃuːz/
鞋子
💡 拿鞋子时说 "Shoes! Left shoe, right shoe!"
"Where are your shoes? Put on your shoes!"
Put on /pʊt ɒn/
穿上
💡 每次穿衣服都说 "Put on your shirt!" 让宝宝理解 put on = 穿上
"Put on your clothes! Let's go outside!"
Put on your shirt! 👕 Put on your shoes! 👟
和宝宝一起说！

🎵 模块四 · 儿歌：Head Shoulders Knees and Toes
🎵 Head Shoulders Knees and Toes
身体儿歌 · 边唱边摸身体部位
Head, shoulders, knees and toes,
头、肩膀、膝盖、脚趾头，
knees and toes!
膝盖和脚趾！
