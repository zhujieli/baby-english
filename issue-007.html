🐻 今天我们来认识一位忙碌的小熊！跟着小熊度过快乐的一天，学学他都忙些什么！

💡 本期目标：认识 5 个动作词汇，学唱 1 首公交车儿歌，掌握小熊很忙故事

📚 模块一 · 今日核心词汇
Busy /ˈbɪzi/
忙碌的
💡 今天很忙！"Little Bear is busy! We are busy too!"
"I am busy! So many things to do!"
Help /help/
帮助
💡 帮助别人时说 "Can you help me? Thank you for your help!"
"I want to help! Let me help!"
Play /pleɪ/
玩
💡 出去玩时说 "Time to play! Let's play outside!"
"Baby wants to play! Play with me!"
Run /rʌn/
跑
💡 宝宝跑的时候说 "Run run run! Fast or slow?"
"I can run! Watch me run!"
Happy /ˈhæpi/
开心的
💡 宝宝笑的时候说 "You are so happy! I'm happy too!"
"Happy happy! We are all happy!"
Little Bear is busy! 🐻 Happy happy!
和宝宝一起说！

🎵 模块四 · 儿歌：The Wheels on the Bus
🚌 The Wheels on the Bus
公交车儿歌 · 边唱边做动作
The wheels on the bus go round and round,
公交车的轮子转呀转，
round and round, round and round.
转呀转，转呀转。
The doors on the bus go open and shut,
公交车的门开呀关，
open and shut, open and shut. 🤲
开呀关，开呀关。
💡 动作提示："round and round" → 双手画圈 ♻️ "open and shut" → 双手开合 🤲 超级适合在公交车上学！
