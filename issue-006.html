📚 模块一 · 今日核心词汇
Breakfast /ˈbrekfəst/
早餐
💡 每天早上第一句："Good morning! It's breakfast time!"
"Breakfast is ready! Come and eat!"
Hungry /ˈhʌŋɡri/
饿的
💡 宝宝没精神时说 "Are you hungry? Let's eat!"
"I'm hungry! I want breakfast!"
Yummy /ˈjʌmi/
好吃的
💡 宝宝吃第一口后问 "Is it yummy?"
"Yummy yummy! This is so delicious!"
Milk /mɪlk/
牛奶
💡 递牛奶时说 "Here's your milk! Drink it up!"
"Milk is good for you! M is for milk!"
Full /fʊl/
吃饱了
💡 宝宝不想吃时说 "Are you full?" 教宝宝用英语表达吃饱
"I'm full! No more, thank you!"
Breakfast time! 🥣 Yummy! I'm full!
和宝宝一起说！
